Aliaksandr Siarohin

Welcome! I am a Ph.D Student at the University of Trento where I work under the supervision of Nicu Sebe at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG). My research interests include machine learning for image animation, video generation, generative adversarial networks and domain adaptation.

My works has been published in top computer vision and machine learning conferences. I also did internship in Snap Inc. and Google. Moreover I'm a Snap Research Fellow in 2020.
